About Ko-matha Dairy Farm

At Ko-matha Dairy Farm, we pride ourselves on delivering fresh and high-quality dairy products. Our Indian A1 cows produce rich and nutritious milk that is transformed into a variety of products including curd, cova-sweet, butter, and ghee. Our farm is committed to sustainable practices and the well-being of our animals.
